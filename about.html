    • St.Mother Theresa Engineering College

    Mother Theresa Engineering College, popularly known as SMTEC, was established in the year 2009 with the vision to empower budding engineers in technical and entrepreneurial training and to contribute to the socio-economic augmentation of the nation.

    Built on the Tirunelveli-Thoothukudi National Expressway and close to the Thoothukudi Airport at Vagaikulam, The St. Mother Theresa Engineering College campus offers easy access from Thoothukudi, Tirunelveli, Tiruchendur, Kovilpatti and towns and villages surrounding these cities. The campus is self-sustained with all the modern facilities required for quality engineering education and a comfortable stay at the campus.

    The campus is dotted with carefully planned and executed landscape with trees and plants that add a shade of green elegance to the campus. The buildings are modern and well appointed. The residential facilities are contemporary and offer the students the comfort of a home-away-from-home.

    The campus breathes the fresh air of nature with a vast green pastures and is a pleasant mix of of a sense of universal brotherhood, tradition, culture and sophistication. The Management carefully preserves and nurtures the campus by maintaining its air, water, land and surroundings free from pollution and litter.

    Students of St. Mother Theresa Engineering College are given the opportunity to pursue first-rate and advanced technical education regardless of background, gender or financial constraints. The necessity to promote most advantageous learning and service is well harmonized and clearly expressed by allowing students to successfully plan their education and competently achieve the education they need. Curricular and co-curricular programs form an integral part of the curriculum and help students to have commanding incorporation of theory and practical knowledge. Each individual is motivated to increase the gravity of responsibility and be committed to serve the nation.

    The college is full of opportunities where students' talents can thrive and it systematically transforms a graduate to a future ready professional. The institution is open to the exchange of ideas, where discovery, creativity, and personal and professional development can flourish. It is a responsive, student-oriented institution that is committed to the creation, dissemination, and acquisition of knowledge through teaching, research and service.
