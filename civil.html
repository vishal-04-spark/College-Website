CIVIL ENGINEERING

The Civil Engineering Department was started in 2010 with the vision to keep pace with the advancing technologies in the field of Civil Engineering and to develop a sense of self-discipline, social responsibility, a co-operative and good work attitude, and play a commendable role in the prosperity of human civilization and protection of human civilization from extinction by contributing to better infrastructure development using the skills acquired during the years of learning.
