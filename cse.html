COMPUTER SCIENCE ENGINEERING

Computer Science Engineers apply the principles of computer science and mathematical analysis to the design, development, testing and evaluation of the software systems. Computer Science Engineers are offered various roles in the industry as Database Analysts, Web Developers, Mobile Applications Developer, Software Testing, Business Intelligence Analyst, Data Architect, System Engineer and Information Technology Manager. Computer Science is an exciting, challenging and growing field that impacts the world and everyday life in countless ways.

The department of Computer Science and Engineering was established in the year 2011. The total sanctioned intake of the CSE department is 60 students, which is empowered by 11 faculty. ZOHO, INFOSYS, CTS, WIPRO, CTS, HCL, Kaar, Virtusa, Intain, Value Labs, Astrazeneca, Gain insights, Aspire Systems, Vedantu, Atlas, Accenture, Infoview and Tech Mahindra Ltd, are some of the prominent placement companies that our students were placed with a good package. Students are encouraged to be a part of professional bodies like IEEE, ISTE,IEEE Computer Society (IEEECS), Computing Research Association and American Mathematical Society.

The department has the tie up with RehHat Linux, and RedHat Linux Powered Laboratory is functioning in the department. Also the department is the member of Computer Society of India.
